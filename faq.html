This app is designed to help teens with autism learn social skills through interactive games and activities.

To get started, simply create an account and explore the different features of the app.

Players get 16 words in a 4 x 4 grid divided into four hidden categories.

Figure out connections between words and group them into sets of four; grouped words become a category.

Players must figure out all categories by grouping the words.

You have four chances to group words into categories.

Feel free to click the chat button, and ask any question. The question, along with your username, will be sent to our headquarters!
