How to Play

Find Words That Match:

You will see many words on the screen, arranged in a square.
Your job is to find 4 words that are similar or belong together.
Think about what the words mean. How are some of them connected?

Select Words:

Click on a word to select it. It will look different when it's selected.
Find and click on 3 more words that are connected to the first word.

Keep Going:

If you were right, those words will group together.
Now, find more groups of words that match.
Keep doing this until you have grouped all the words.

You Get Four Tries:

You can try to group words together up to 4 times.
If you make a mistake, it's okay. Try again!
If you use all 4 tries, you have to wait until the next day to play again.
